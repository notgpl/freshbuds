Our Story

Crafting Excellence
Since 2008

Since 2008, Fresh Buds Caterers & Events has been known as one of the best catering services in Kottayam. We have established a reputation for exceptional food, creativity and presentation combined with outstanding service. Fresh Buds provides full-service catering, party planning and event management — from corporate entertaining and business luncheons to weddings, receptions and live music events. Our experienced event planners custom design each menu and coordinate both the décor and food presentation for your event. Our team of well-mannered professionals ensures guests feel informed and cared for, making every event truly memorable — all within your budget and preferences.
